- Using shiny paper or film over metal parts was proposed as a cost-effective way to achieve a premium look without expensive shiny components.

### **Market and Business Context**
- The machine targets production of “gun badges,” often for anime/game merchandise (B2C).
- High productivity is needed; some companies pair such machines with robot arms for full automation.
- A participant’s company is a printing firm in Fuchu City, Tokyo, and offered a factory tour.
- 三幸商事 plans bulk sales of parts (1,000–2,000 units), differing from competitors selling in smaller lots (e.g., 100).
